#Reg-Ex

/reg-ex/ - between //
^ - start
$ - end
[] - group of char
(?:)  - non capturing group
? - token before ? is optional
+ - group before + can repeat infinite times
{2} - group before + can repeat 2 times
\s - any type of space
0-9 - numbers
\d - digites
A-Za-z - A to Z or a to z




examples
1) /^(?:10|20|30)+$/ - value should be 10 or 20 or 30
2) /^(?:1)?(?:[0-9])?(?:[0-9])$/ - min 0 max 199 integer
3) /^(?:[0-5])?(?:[0-9])$/ - 0 to 59 range integer
